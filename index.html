Why Langorine?

Reading Skills

Our library features French reading materials for learners at all levels. With our parallel text methodology, learners can challenge themselves by reading more advanced stories while still understanding the meaning.

Building Vocabulary

Learn new words everyday. Enhance your vocabulary and grammar through interactive multiple-choice quizzes, fill-in-the-blank activities, and engaging flashcards designed for each level, beginner to advanced.

Parallel Translation

Not sure about a word? With our translation feature, you can view translations alongside the original French text. This allows beginners to understand stories easily while gradually transitioning to reading fully in French.

Your Journey Starts Here.

Our library of French stories and articles offers something for every learner, from complete beginners to advanced readers. Explore engaging short stories, cultural topics, and news written for different language levels. Each text is designed with clear language and helpful vocabulary to help you improve your reading and comprehension skills in French.
