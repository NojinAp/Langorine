Frequently Asked Questions

What is Langorine?

Langorine is a platform designed to help learners improve their French through engaging reading materials. Users can explore stories, articles, and poems tailored to different proficiency levels.

How do I choose my level?

Langorine uses three learning levels: Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced. If you are new to French, start with Beginner. Intermediate learners can choose Intermediate, while more experienced learners can explore Advanced content. You can choose or change your level after signing in.

What types of content are available?

Each level includes a variety of reading materials such as short stories, articles, and poems. These texts are designed to help you improve vocabulary, comprehension, and reading fluency.

Can I see the translation while reading?

Yes. Langorine allows users to view translations alongside the original French text, helping learners understand new vocabulary while staying engaged with the story.

Can I test my knowledge?

Yes. Users can test their knowledge by doing features such as flashcards, multiple choice and fill-in-the-blank quizzes.

Is Langorine suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. Langorine offers content starting at A1 level, making it accessible for complete beginners who want to start reading French.

Do I need an account to use Langorine?

Yes. All features are only available to users; however, everything is 100% free.

How often is new content added?

New stories and articles are added regularly to provide fresh reading material for learners at all levels.

Can I access Langorine on mobile devices?

Yes. Langorine is designed to work on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, allowing you to practice reading anytime.

Who created Langorine?

Langorine was designed and developed by PixelATE as a platform to support independent French learners.
